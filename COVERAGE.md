# Coverage

## What Is Included

- **Miljoeknologi** -- Investment support for barn technology reducing environmental impact (slurry acidification, chemical air cleaning, covering). Up to 40% co-financing.
- **Oekologisk Arealtilskud** -- Area payments for organic farming. Conversion (1,050 DKK/ha) and maintenance (870 DKK/ha) subsidies, plus specialty crop supplement (1,200 DKK/ha).
- **Pleje af graes- og naturarealer** -- Area payments for extensive management of protected nature areas. Grazing (2,600 DKK/ha), mowing (1,800 DKK/ha), special care (3,200 DKK/ha).
- **Tilskud til skovrejsning** -- Investment support for afforestation. Min. 75% broadleaf, 20-year forest obligation. Priority near drinking water resources.
- **Tilskud til minivaadomraader** -- Up to 100% establishment support for constructed wetlands removing nitrogen. Annual maintenance payment.
- **Vaadomraader og lavbundsprojekter** -- Project support for wetland establishment for nitrogen removal and carbon sequestration.
- **Moderniseringsstoette** -- Investment in primary agricultural production. Extra points for young farmers (<40 years).
- **Oe-tilskud til smaaeer** -- Special operating and investment support for farming on 27 Danish small islands.
- **Dyrevelfaerdstilskud** -- Subsidies for free-range and organic animal husbandry with improved animal welfare.
- **Energieffektivisering i landbruget** -- Investment support for energy-saving technology (LED, heat pumps, insulation, solar panels). Up to 40%.
- **Tilskud til biogasanlaeg** -- Establishment support for communal and farm biogas plants. Feed-in tariff for electricity and heat.
- **Klima-lavbundsprojekter** -- Withdrawal of low-lying soils from farming. Carbon sequestration and greenhouse gas reduction. One-time compensation.
- **12 grant schemes, 24 eligible items, 10 stacking rules, 27 application guidance steps**
- **Stacking rules** -- Which grants can and cannot be combined, with conditions
- **Application guidance** -- Step-by-step process for each grant with evidence requirements and Tast Selv portal links
- **Deadline tracking** -- Open, upcoming, and rolling grant windows

## What Is NOT Included

- **Grundbetaling (Basic Payment Scheme)** -- Direct CAP payments, separate from targeted grant schemes
- **SFI-equivalent eco-schemes** -- Danish eco-scheme payments under CAP SP not yet covered individually
- **Regional/municipal grants** -- Some municipalities offer additional agricultural support
- **Real-time application status** -- This is reference data, not connected to the Tast Selv portal
- **Exact budget allocations** -- Budgets vary per round and are published by Landbrugsstyrelsen

## Known Gaps

1. Application periods vary per scheme -- check lbst.dk for current rounds
2. Payment rates may be adjusted annually under the CAP Strategic Plan
3. Some schemes have additional requirements not captured (e.g., environmental impact assessments)
4. Stacking rules are based on current published guidance and may change between grant rounds
